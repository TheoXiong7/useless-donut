Disco mode strobes.

This mode uses rapid flashing lights, high-contrast color shifts, and screen shake that intensify with the music.

  • Do not use if you have photosensitive epilepsy or a history of seizures triggered by flashing lights.
  • Stop immediately if you feel dizzy, disoriented, or unwell.
  • View at a comfortable distance in a well-lit room.

You can dial down the intensity in the Tweaks panel after enabling.
